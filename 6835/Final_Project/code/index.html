Instructions

Voice commands

  • "Play" - plays video
  • "Pause"/"stop" - pauses video
  • "Rewind" - rewinds video 10 seconds
  • "Fast forward" - fast forward video 10 seconds
  • "Slow mo"/"slow down" - plays video at 0.5 speed

Gesture Commands

  • Palm forward - toggles play/pause
  • Swipe right to left with right hand - rewinds video 10 seconds
  • Swipe left to right with left hand - fast forward video 10 seconds
  • Swipe down - plays video at 0.5 speed

Drawing

  • Point with index finger to find cursor
  • Extend thumb to start drawing!
